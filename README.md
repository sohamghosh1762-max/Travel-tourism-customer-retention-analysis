# 📌 Overview

The **Travel & Hospitality Analytics Intelligence System** is a data-driven analytics and machine learning project designed to optimize customer retention, reduce booking cancellations, and improve revenue management within the hospitality industry.

The system combines:
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Predictive Analytics
- Business Intelligence
- Data Visualization

to generate actionable hospitality business insights.

---

# 🚀 Business Objectives

- Analyze hotel booking behavior
- Predict booking cancellations
- Identify seasonal demand patterns
- Optimize Average Daily Rate (ADR)
- Improve customer retention strategies
- Support data-driven business decisions

# 📈 Key Insights

- Longer lead times increase cancellation probability
- Seasonal demand impacts pricing significantly
- Non-refundable deposits reduce cancellations
- Customer segments exhibit different booking behaviors
- ADR varies across seasons and market segments
